HypnoDisc

HypnoDisc is the name for both this library and the hardware it was made for.

The hardware consists of a small wooden disc where a number of LEDs are mounted in a circle. In the case of our prototype, this is a 24-light disc. These LEDs are driven by a small attached board that contains a chain of 74C595 8-bit shift registers.
